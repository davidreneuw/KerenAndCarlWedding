How we met

We met through a chance encounter while working at a children’s summer camp in 2016. Little did we anticipate that our initial meeting would lead to a deep and enduring love. Amidst the excitement of catching Pokémon and exploring the great outdoors that summer, we found solace in each other's company and quickly realized that we shared something special. Seven years have flown by, and we've been blessed by God's grace through every peak and valley. Join us as we celebrate our love story, built on a foundation of friendship, shared passions, and endless love. We cannot wait to begin this new chapter surrounded by the ones we hold dear, as we say 'I do' and begin our journey together.

Q & A
Where and when should I RSVP?

Please RSVP at your earliest convenience through our With Joy website to confirm your attendance. The final RSVP deadline is July 12, 2024.

Do you have a wedding registry? What gifts should I bring?

We are so excited to celebrate our wedding with you all, so your presence will be more than enough. However, if you would like to support us with a gift towards our future, there will be a card box at the reception for any monetary contributions.

Can I bring a +1?

Please click the RSVP button and enter your name to see if a plus one has been included in your invitation.

Are kids welcome?

As much as we would love to celebrate with your little ones, invited children will be restricted to the bride and groom’s close family.

Where will I park?

Parking is available at the venue, free of charge. Please follow the signs to the parking lot.

Is the wedding / reception indoors or outdoors?

The wedding ceremony and reception will be indoors, however, there is a private terrace and a cozy firepit adjacent to the reception area for our guests to enjoy, weather permitting!
