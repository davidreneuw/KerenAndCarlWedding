Q & A
When should I RSVP?

Please RSVP at your earliest convenience through our With Joy website to confirm your attendance. The final RSVP deadline is July 12, 2024.

Do you have a wedding registry? What gifts should I bring?

Although we will not be creating a wedding registry, there will be a card box at the reception for monetary gifts. We are also accepting e-transfers to clucasan1@gmail.com to kickstart our future together!

Can I bring a +1?

Please click the RSVP button and enter your name to see if a plus one has been included in your invitation.

Are kids/pets welcome?

As much as we would love to celebrate with your little ones, invited children will be restricted to the bride and groom’s close family.

Where will I park?

Parking is available at the venue, free of charge. Please follow the signs to the parking lot.

Is the wedding / reception indoors or outdoors?

The wedding ceremony and reception will be indoors, however, there is a private terrace and a cozy firepit adjacent to the reception area for our guests to enjoy, weather permitting!
